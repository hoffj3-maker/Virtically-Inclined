Step-by-Step Process:

Step 1: Complete Your Prerequisites

Before booking or visiting, all participants must complete a waiver
  • All adults (18 years and older) must fill out a waiver and provide ID
  • Anyone under 18 years of age requires the waiver to be signed by a parent or legal guardian
Step 2: Select Your Climbing Type Below ↓
    ★ Your choice depends on your experience level

Step 3: Schedule Your Visit

★ Booking is strongly recommended for all climbers planning to visit before 4 pm on weekdays, as school groups often book these slots.
1: Select Date and Time: On the next screen, select ‘Information and Booking’. Choose the day you would like to book and your desired timeslot.
2. Capacity Check:
  • If the spot is simply labelled as ‘Available’, there are at least 10 spots left.
  • If a number is displayed, it indicates how many spots remain to meet full capacity across all booking types.
3. Visit Duration: Although the system may show a 2.5 hour slot, this is solely an approximation for gauging capacity; there is no time limit for Bouldering or Roped Climbing visits.
4. Guarantee Your Spot: Making a booking guarantees your spot as long as you arrive within the first 15 minutes of the booking time.

Step 4: Review Booking Deadlines

Booking timeslots are subject to the following deadlines:
  • Bouldering, Climbing Adventure, and Roped Climbing: Can be booked up to 2 hours ahead of the start time.
  • Morning Climbing: Please book 12 hours in advance.
  • Birthday/Small Group Bookings: Must be made a minimum of 48 hours ahead of the program time.
If you are interested in coming in during times blocked off for school groups, please call 780-496-9390 for details and options.

Step 5: Complete and Pay for Your Booking

Complete the booking information on the final screen:
  • Participant Details: Input the number of participants and provide names and dates of birth for all climbers (for proper identification in the system)
  • Passes: Indicate the type of pass you have (such as a single visit, multi-visit, or punch pass). Multi-visit or punch passes can be purchased online
  • Required Information: Answer all questions to ensure appropriate products are provided (e.g. kids, students, needing rental gear)
  • Payment: All purchases must be paid for online at the time of booking
